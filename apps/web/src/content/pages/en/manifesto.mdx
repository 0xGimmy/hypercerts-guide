Over the past three years since the founding of FAB DAO (Formosa Art Bank), we confidently assert: "Technology cannot change the status quo, but trust can." FAB DAO has utilized various new technologies to support many nonprofit projects, primarily focusing on blockchain, cryptocurrency, and art NFTs. We believe these decentralized tools can strengthen the social network and foster the growth of positive initiatives.

After collaborating with open-minded DAO friends on diverse topics such as environmental education, local revitalization, alternative learning, artistic creation, and charitable actions, we can firmly state that technology alone is insufficient. The advanced tools cannot replace human connections. In the past, educating our partners about NFTs was a huge challenge. The term "education" itself carries a top-down mindset, which we want to avoid. These extra efforts have clarified the essence of technology for us.

Good technology should help us build trust, making us willing to spend time and contribute to projects that align with our values and beliefs. In extreme situations, it should provide us with greater resilience, allowing us to "together" swiftly restore our society. This is the essence of the web3 public sphere, the answer FAB DAO found through decentralized governance.

The trust we seek is not just about words but about actions, suffering, and shared glory. Digital trust is no different from real-world trust. Moreover, terms like public goods, Harberger tax, and quadratic voting are academically impressive but often hinder mutual understanding and collaboration. Let's hide these fancy concepts and let trust naturally emerge through real actions.

We are tired of repeating the same words and dreams in numerous forums, seminars, and conferences.

My friends! Let's feel the pain of sprained ankles.
My friends! Let's roll up our sleeves and get to work.
My friends! Let's act!

How can we strengthen trust?
Our current answer is to unite through HyperTogatherings!
This is the **Taiwan HyperAwesome! Guidebook**.

Whether you are a working professional, retiree, student, or nonprofit worker, we all have a bit of time, energy, creativity, and money to support what we love, but this requires trust. Without trust, asking for money is impossible; with trust, people will join in without hesitation.

The concept is simple: we believe the most feasible and pragmatic action is "Togathering." What kind of project done by friends are you willing to join? We need your sincere recommendation. A recommendation is the beginning of a togathering, whether it's for brainstorming, fundraising, or assisting in project, such as creating NFTs to preserve beautiful things.

Thus, we created the "**Taiwan HyperAwesome! Guidebook**".
It is a straightforward webpage where you can sincerely recommend projects, actions, or organizations you love, writing down your reasons with your name. Try to gather people to get to know them, so your other friends can also be willing to join after seeing your recommendation. Trust starts from here.

We've also adopted some small tools on the "Taiwan HyperAwesome! Guidebook" page. If you're really curious, read on. We've introduced a digital certificate, "HyperAwesome," as a blue checkmark. If a project gains enough trust, the team will award a blue checkmark to this "sincere recommendation." Additionally, to prevent anyone from wanting to donate with cryptocurrency, we've designed a quadratic funding mechanism, allowing you to donate to your favorite actions proportionately in one go. FAB DAO also has a "Green Sofa" team responsible for raising public funds in cryptocurrency from around the world. These funds can be used to repurchase projects they deem impactful, creating a positive cycle.

Who can recommend? Anyone can recommend, but let's start with those we know! Give a sincere shoutout to those you want to act with. A sincere recommendation is just the first step, letting us know what issues you and I care about and are willing to invest in. Whether during major events or in everyday efforts, it's a good way to open the window of mutual cooperation.

"Taiwan HyperAwesome! Guidebook" welcomes you to play together!
